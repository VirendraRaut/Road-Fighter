🎯 Objective

  • Avoid enemy vehicles
  • Collect fuel canisters to keep driving
  • Grab coins for bonus score
  • Survive as long as possible

📊 Scoring

  • +1 point per frame survived
  • +50 points per coin collected
  • High score saved automatically

❤️ Lives

Start with 3 lives. Each crash removes one. Zero lives = Game Over.

⛽ Fuel

Fuel drains constantly. Collect yellow fuel canisters. Empty tank = Game Over.

⚡ Difficulty

Every 300 points the road gets faster, enemies multiply, and the challenge increases. Stay sharp!
